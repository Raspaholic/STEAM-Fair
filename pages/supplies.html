Supplies to Make Kombucha and SCOBY leather

These are the supplies I used for my STEAM Fair experiment to make the Kombucha. Food dye or water soluble dye is optional as I didn't buy or use it in my experiment. The dye could be added during the SCOBY production to dye the SCOBY for the leather.

Learn More

1 Plastic Bin with lid

The plastic bin is what I used to make the Kombucha in to have a wide enough space to yield more SCOBY.

2 Seedling germination heatpads

The seedling germination heat pads are used to keep a constant temperature around 75 F to create the best environment for the SCOBY growth.

Pack of cooking skewers

The cooking skewers are only used to create a space for air circulation at the top of the bin near the lid.

92% Rubbing Alcohol

The rubbing alcohol is used as a disinfectant and a control for the experiment as to not alter the production of SCOBY with a non-disinfected brewing container.

Pair of gloves

The pair of gloves are disinfected with rubbing alcohol and used for the handling of the SCOBY and replenishing of tea.

Spray Bottle

The spray bottle is used to spray and disinfect any utensil or supply used in the production of SCOBY with rubbing alcohol.

Soft Brush
