Welcome the Leather of the Future!

The leather industry is very polluting and the aim of this project is to help spotlight a new natural leather alternative. 'Kombucha Leather' (SCOBY) is a leather alternative which doesn't pollute as much compared to the leather industry today.

Learn More

100% Biodegradable

SCOBY leather is 100% biodegradable! You could compost SCOBY leather which has a minimal and short lived impact on the environment compared to animal-based leather, which endues a long decomposition process that releases chemicals from the tanning process.

Eco-Friendly

SCOBY leather doesn't go through the same tanning process as traditional leather does which means it doesn't require chemicals! Traditional leather tanning requires non-biodegradable chemicals and the tanning process emits toxic gasses. Traditional leather tanning pollutes water from leather factories which is released into rivers.

No Animal Harm

SCOBY leather's growing process is made entirely from a cellulose film produced by the bacteria while animals are killed for their hyde. SCOBY grows rapidly which saves time and resources compared to traditional leather-making.

Extremely Versatile

SCOBY leather can be grown into any shape and doesn't require sewing when sticking pieces together since they glue stick together, drying as one. Animal leather can't compete with the versatility of SCOBY leather and is bound to the constraints of the animal its hyde is coming from.

Production Costs

You only need sweetened tea to grow SCOBY, making it cheaper to produce or manufacture than animal hyde. Animal products are more resource intensive compared to SCOBY leather.
